ABOUT ME

Welcome to my creative page, I am My and you are looking at my creative works.
As early as I was just a few years old, I discovered that I love drawing and passionate about aesthetics. I spent my days drawing and reading illustration books. When I graduated from high school, I knew I want to be a graphic designer. Graduated with a degree of Design in Visual Communication in 2009, I starting my creative career with enthusiasm to make an unique impact to the design world. It has been nearly 10 years and still with that same old mindset,
I am in searching for more challenges through new workplace environments where I could make my small contributions and to grow indefinitely as an graphic professional.

Take some time to browse through my works. If you sync, I would love to connect with you on a professional bond. Together we will discover the beauty and meaning in every part of life.
